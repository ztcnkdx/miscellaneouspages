Beer Game

Game Rules & Operational Manual

Core Objective

Minimize your Total Cumulative Cost over 25 weeks. The team with the lowest total supply chain cost wins.

Standard Operating Costs

  • Holding Inventory: $0.50 per unit / week.
  • Backlog (Out of Stock): $1.00 per unit / week.

Supply Chain Physics

  • Flow: Factory ‚ûú Distributor ‚ûú Wholesaler ‚ûú Retailer ‚ûú Customer.
  • Orders: Transmitted upstream immediately (0 delay).
  • Shipments: Standard transit time is 1 week (arrive next turn).
  • Progression: The week advances immediately once all human players have submitted their orders.

Strategic Services (The Shop)

Spend money to save money. Services are added to your weekly cost.

  • Expedite Shipping ($50): Reduces lead time to 0 weeks. Incoming shipments arrive this current week. One-time use.
  • Demand Forecast ($75): Reveals the exact Customer Demand for the next 5 weeks.
  • Neighbor Visibility ($100): Reveals Inventory/Backlog of your direct Supplier and Customer for 5 weeks.
  • Global Visibility ($150): Reveals real-time data for the entire supply chain for 5 weeks.
